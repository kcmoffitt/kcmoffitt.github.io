Evaluation checklist

  • Define the claim before choosing the metric.
  • Preserve test-set independence and document leakage checks.
  • Segment results by relevant populations, contexts, and use cases.
  • Record acceptance criteria before reviewing outcomes.
  • Track limitations and failure examples alongside aggregate scores.

Audit focus

The auditor should be able to trace each public or internal performance claim to a test design, data source, result, reviewer, and decision record.
