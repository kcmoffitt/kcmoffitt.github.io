Governance

Governance checks

  • AI use cases are inventoried, classified, and assigned to accountable owners.
  • Policies define prohibited uses, approval gates, human oversight, and change triggers.
  • Standards and regulations are mapped to controls and evidence.
  • Monitoring, incidents, and exceptions reach the right governance forum.

Audit focus

Governance is working when decisions are traceable, owners are real, control evidence exists, and exceptions do not disappear into informal channels.
