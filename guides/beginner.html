AI Audit Foundations

Audit the claim

AI systems arrive wrapped in claims: accurate, fair, safe, secure, explainable, compliant, monitored. The audit starts by separating those claims and asking what evidence would make each one believable.

Minimum evidence set

  • System owner, business purpose, users, and affected parties.
  • Model inventory entry with version, vendor, training data summary, and deployment context.
  • Documented intended use, prohibited use, limitations, and escalation paths.
  • Evaluation results, test design, acceptance criteria, and known gaps.
  • Monitoring plan for drift, incidents, overrides, and periodic review.
