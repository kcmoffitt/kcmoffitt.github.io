Risk

Model Risk

Scoping questions

  • What decision or workflow does the model influence?
  • What happens when the model is wrong, unavailable, manipulated, or misused?
  • Who can accept residual risk, and where is that acceptance recorded?
  • What changes require revalidation before release?

Control areas

Strong model-risk programs connect inventory, validation, change management, monitoring, incident response, and independent review. Auditors should test whether those controls operate in the deployed context, not only on paper.
